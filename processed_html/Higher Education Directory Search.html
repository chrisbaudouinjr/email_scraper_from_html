Former U.S. Attorney General Alberto Gonzales to Become Dean of Belmont U College of Law
Monday, April 7, 2014 - Belmont University

Judge Alberto Gonzales, former U.S. Attorney General, will become the new dean of Belmont University's College of Law. He will replace founding dean Jeff Kinsler who has decided to become a full-time faculty member. Judge Gonzales will begin in his new position on June 1st.

Belmont University College of Law receives provisional accreditation
Thursday, June 13, 2013 - Nashville Business Journal

Belmont's College of Law has received provisional accreditation from the American Bar Association (ABA). According to the ABA, provisional accreditation entitles law schools to all rights of fully accredited law schools. Law schools must be provisionally accredited for two years before applying for full accreditation.

American Baptist College in Nashville joins list of historically black colleges
Monday, April 15, 2013 - The Tennessean

The American Baptist College in Nashville is now on the list of historically black colleges. It will now be eligible for funding and grants that are a result of that designation. As a result, American Baptist received a gift of $100,000 from Belmont University.
